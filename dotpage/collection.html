Let Ricing Linux

Arch, Manjaro, EndeavourOS, NixOS, and Arctic all share the same family lineage, as they are based on the Linux kernel and fall within the Arch-based or independent distributions with similar philosophies. Likewise, Linux Mint, Pop OS Ubuntu are part of the same ecosystem, both being based on Debian and sharing much of the same infrastructure and package management system.
